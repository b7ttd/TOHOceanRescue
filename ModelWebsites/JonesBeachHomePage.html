Lt. Paul Lundwall Passing

Tuesday, 17 November 2020 19:37 administrator

It is with deep regret that the Jones Beach Lifeguard Corps announce the passing of Lt. Paul Lundwall. Paul served as a lifeguard with the JBLC for 50+ seasons. His name and face was synonymous with all that it meant to be a Jones Beach Lifeguard. He served in many capacities as both a past Union President & lifeguard officer, retiring as the Lieutenant of RM5. "On time, In uniform, Watch the water." He passed down his love for the beach and dedication of saving lives to all his children, Britt, Kim and Chad all who also served as members of the JBLC. Our hearts are heavy for the Lundwall family and our deepest condolences go out to them during this time.

Services will be as follows:
Charles G. Schmitt Funeral Home
3863 Merrick Road
Seaford, NY 11783
Thursday, November 19th 6pm-9pm
Friday, November 20th 2-5pm and 7pm-9pm.
*A service will take place at 4pm on Friday at Schmitt’s funeral home.

Annual Winter Party 2020

Sunday, 19 January 2020 17:01 administrator

Come celebrate with the Jones Beach Lifeguard Corps at our annual winter celebration on Sunday, January 26th, 4:30PM to 7:00PM at Small Craft Brewery located at 66 Merrick Road in Amityville, Free Beer, Wine, Soda and BBQ food for all union dues paying members. Non union guests are $10 per person.

The JBLC will also be making a charitable donation to Island Harvest, so we ask that everyone bring a non perishable item or canned food to throw in our donation box. In addition there will be a 50/50 raffle with proceeds going to fight the Australian Wildfires as well as a separate raffle for a brand new custom made Jones Beach Surfboard! You're def not gonna wanna miss this one! Surfboard raffle ticket costs: $10 each or you can buy 3 for $25, 6 for $50 or 12 for $100.

*Small Craft Brewery is hidden behind Sammy's Auto Repair. People can park in front of Sammy's auto or in back. If not park on Ketchum Ave and walk to the back of Sammy's Auto shop.

Hope to see you there!
